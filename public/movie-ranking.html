Top 3 Movies According to Xander

My favorite movies of all-time.

Detroit Rock City

Released in 1999. Written by Carl V. Dupre and directed by Adam Rifkin.

White Chicks

Released in 2004. Screenplay by Xavier Cook, Andy McElfresh, Michael Anthony Snowden, Keenan Ivory Wayans, Marlon Wayans, Shawn Wayans. Directed by Keenan Ivory Wayans.

Avengers: Infinity War

Released in 2018. Screenplay by Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely. Directed by Joe Russo and Anthony Russo.
